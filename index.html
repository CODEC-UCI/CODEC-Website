Who We Are

Community of Diverse Empowerment in Computing

Our Mission

CODEC is a new community space with an emphasis on making tech accessible to everyone. By sharing resources, supporting one another, and celebrating our diverse experiences in computing, we are able to foster a welcoming environment for all!

What We Do

We host events and workshops to bring the computing community together. We want to create a supportive environment where everyone has access to resources and opportunities.

Who Can Join

No matter your background or experience level, anyone interested in computing and community building is welcome!
